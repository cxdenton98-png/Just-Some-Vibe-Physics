Just Some Vibe Physics

Interactive physics explorations & visualizations

About These Projects: These projects are my personal explorations of physics concepts through self-learning and "vibe coding". They do involve considerable usage of LLMs. However, I do make significant effort to keep things in line with known physics. The nature of these simulations are to be exploratory and fun!
